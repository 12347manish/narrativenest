# Privacy Policy for NarrativeNest

**Effective Date:** April 22, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 22, 2026

---

## Overview

NarrativeNest is a personal watchlist and reading list app that helps you keep track of movies and books you want to watch or read. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

**Short version: we collect nothing. We don't even have a server.**

---

## Information We Collect

**None.**

NarrativeNest does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. The app:

- Does not require an account or sign-in
- Does not ask for your name, email, or any personal details
- Does not track your location
- Does not use analytics or crash reporting services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not communicate with any external servers

---

## Data Storage

Any data you create within NarrativeNest (your lists, titles, notes) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is fully under your control
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

---

## Third-Party Services

NarrativeNest does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Since NarrativeNest collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or any age).

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version of the app adds features that involve data), we will update the effective date above and note what changed.
